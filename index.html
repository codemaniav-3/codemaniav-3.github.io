CodeMania v2.0 is a 6-hour long algorithmic coding competition which is the fourth and final project of the “Month of CodeGenix'' project series, organized by the IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of SLTC. It is the first-ever algorithmic coding competition held at SLTC. The competition is scheduled to be held on the 12th of June 2021 from 6.00 pm onwards for 6 hours, through HackerRank and Zoom platforms. CodeMania v2.0 is only open for SLTC undergraduates, and teams of three (3) will compete to solve problem sets of varying difficulty levels in the time provided to them. Contestants can use any programming language to solve the problems provided. Registrations for CodeMania v2.0 will be open from the 30th of May 2021 till the 9th of June 2021.

CodeMania v2.0 is guided by an advisory panel headed by Dr. Dhammika Elkaduwa, the head of the School of IT & Computing, as the chief advisor. The panel of judges of CodeMania v2.0 will be led by research assistant Mr. Suren Sritharan. Lecturers from both SLTC and the University of Peradeniya have collaborated to create the problem sets of this competition.

CodeMania v2.0 mainly intends on giving the undergraduates of SLTC the experience of a coding competition, motivating them to take part in the IEEEXtreme coding competition, and encouraging them to lean towards algorithmic programming. The top 10 teams will receive a Swag Pack (including the official CodeMania v2.0 T-shirts) along with the following cash prizes and consolation gifts.

About


CodeMania v3.0 is an 6-hours long algorithmic coding competition. It will be the final project of the Month of CodeGeniX project series organized by the IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of Sri Lanka Technological Campus. Competitors of CodeMania v3.0 are required to solve problem sets utilizing their problem-solving and risk-taking abilities. The competition is planned to be held online via the HackerRank platform and will be based on the Python programming language. CodeMania v3.0 is open only for undergraduates of the Sri Lanka Technological Campus (SLTC).

CodeMania v3.0 is guided by an advisory panel headed by Dr Dhammika Elkaduwa as the chief advisor. The panel of judges of CodeMania v3.0 will be led by Mr. Suren Sritharan.This is planned to be held on 12 th June, 6 pm onwards via Hacker Rank Platform for SLTC undergraduates.
