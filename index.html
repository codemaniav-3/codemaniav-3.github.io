The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of SLTC introduced CodeMania v3.0 as the project of the Month of CodeGeniX 2022 project series, aiming to provide a platform for the SLTC undergraduates to show their coding skills logically and creatively within a competitive mindset. Therefore, we intend to commence the whole project on the 20th of October 2022, gathering all IT enthusiasts in SLTC, and providing a physical hackathon competition experience for the first time in SLTC history. Moreover, we planned to launch this CodeMania v3.0 code competition, as a 12-hour-long algorithmic coding Hackathon and planned to check our competitors’ (Only the SLTC Undergraduates), coding knowledge in any coding language, providing 10 to 12 problem sets in every hour via the HackerRank platform. Therefore, with the “Codemania v3.0” project, we hope to level up the CodeMania hackathon series to one of the main hackathons in Sri Lanka within the next three years and hope to provide more opportunities for the SLTC undergraduates to step into the IT industry.
